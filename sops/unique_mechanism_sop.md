# SOP: Unique Mechanism Creation for Outbound Outreach

**1. What Is a Unique Mechanism?**
A Unique Mechanism is the specific way your client achieves results that sets them apart from competitors. It's not just what they do but how they do it differently.

**Formula:**
"We got [X result] by using [Unique Mechanism]."

**Example:**
"We helped a SaaS brand scale from $50k to $200k MRR by using trigger-based email and SMS outreach, targeting CMOs who need additional support."

**2. Framework - How to Identify & Create a Unique Mechanism**

**Step 1: Extract Key Actions From Client Case Studies & Onboarding form**
- Look at case studies or onboarding forms to list out all the key steps that drive client results.
- Example: We redesign websites, then run ads on Meta, Google, and TikTok.

**Step 2: Identify the 80/20 Actions**
- Find 1-3 core things that generate 80% of the results (Pareto Principle).
- Example: “When we launch TikTok Shop Ads, we see the most results.”

**Step 3: Simplify & Make It Understandable**
- Explain the mechanism as if you're talking to a child—avoid jargon.
- Example: “We put your products directly into the TikTok shop so customers can buy straight from TikTok without leaving the app.”

**Good vs. Bad Unique Mechanisms (Examples)**

| Good Unique Mechanisms | Bad Unique Mechanisms |
|---|---|
| "We use trigger-based outreach that detects buying signals before your competitors." | "We do AI-driven outreach to get you more leads." |
| "We focus on CMOs who need additional support to maximize existing lead flow." | "We generate sales meetings for CMOs." |
| "Instead of more meetings, we optimize your pipeline to increase deal closes." | "We get you more booked calls." |
