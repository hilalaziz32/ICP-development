**Prepared by:** Scaletopia

---

## Client Summary

Kynship is a boutique DTC growth agency (18–20 clients by design) specializing in Meta/Google paid media, high-volume influencer seeding, UGC/CGC creative production, and P&L-first financial forecasting. Their differentiation is not "better ads" — it's starting with the brand's margin profile, CAC tolerance, and payback window *before* touching the ad account. They use cost controls, not daily budgets, to create profitable scale rather than chase platform metrics. Their flagship proof point is Purdy & Figg: from £450k to £50M+ (100x) in 4 years. Other S-tier wins include Supergut ($100k → $1M/month NCR in 3 months), Black Girl Vitamins ($8M → $85M/year), and Wildbird ($1.8M → $20M NCR over 3 years).

1. **12-Month Financial Projection Guide** — models CAC, retention, margins, and scenarios using brand's own numbers. Position as "the audit that never lies — it's just your bank account."

### Positioning Note on "Free Audit"
Kynship's audit is actually a full competitive P&L analysis (8–10 hours of man-hours). The current framing ("audit") triggers switching off. **Recommended reframe:** "12-Month Performance Forecast" or "Profit-First Growth Model" — positions the offer as a *deliverable* rather than a diagnostic.
