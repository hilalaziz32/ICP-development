## **Template \#1: Revenue Transformation (E-commerce/DTC)**

### **Playbook Components:**

**TRUST Elements:**

* Named company case study: Real brand name with credibility  
* Specific revenue transformation: $X to $Y with timeframe  
* Unique mechanism hint: Specific enough to be believable, broad enough for curiosity

**HUMAN Elements:**

* Pattern interrupt: "I know this is random" (acknowledges cold outreach)  
* Self-aware bridge: "bit of a weird approach😂" (conversational, disarming)  
* Casual CTA: "lmk if it's worth exploring" (texting language, not formal)  
* Flexible timing: "sometime next week" (soft, non-aggressive)

### **Template Version:**

Text 1: Hi {{first\_name}}, it's {{sender\_name}} from {{sender\_company}}. I know this is random but {{revenue\_case\_study\_transformation}} using {{unique\_mechanism}}

Text 2: It's a bit of a weird approach😂 but I think it could work for {{company\_name}} \- lmk if it's worth exploring sometime next week

### **Winning Example:**

Text 1: Hi \[FirstName\], it's Courtney from Chamber. I know this is random but I took Transparent Labs from $2m to $26m/yr in under 2 years using humor to make their ads actually entertaining

Text 2: It's a bit of a weird approach😂 but I think it could work for {company name} \- lmk if it's worth exploring sometime next week

**Industry:** E-commerce/DTC Brands

## **LLM Usage Instructions:**

**Pattern Matching Priority:**

1. **Match Industry \+ Sophistication** to select appropriate template  
2. **Copy the Playbook Components** (Trust \+ Human elements)  
3. **Use the Template Structure** with proper variables  
4. **Mirror the Winning Example** tone and language patterns

**Key Principles:**

* **Trust \= Specific proof \+ Relevance \+ Appropriate tone**  
* **Human \= Pattern interrupts \+ Casual language \+ Soft CTAs**  
* **Every template shows both principles in action**

**Industry-Sophistication Matching:**

* **E-commerce/DTC**: Templates \#1, \#8, \#9, \#11 (casual, growth-focused)  
* **B2B Tech/SaaS**: Templates \#2, \#7 (high sophistication, technical)  
* **Local Services**: Templates \#3, \#4, \#10, \#12 (practical, direct)  
* **Agencies**: Template \#6 (peer-level, insider knowledge)  
* **Trade Services**: Template \#5 (opportunity-focused, practical)

Use the winning examples as your north star for tone, language, and psychology.
