# Email script creation checklist:

- Does the email incorporate the language of why it’s relevant to them? (why them/why now?)
- Does the email sound like you’re from the same industry as the prospect with specific/nuanced lingo?
- When you read the email out loud, does it sound like a manual email from a human?
- Does the email “flow” well and is smooth to read?
- Would you respond to this email?
- Does the email show how it’s different to all the other outbound emails to invoke curiosity? (checklist below)
  - Case Study that shows they’re competent/showing authority to the claims we’re making
  - Unique Mechanism on how they’re different
  - Some level of pattern interrupt → Using Clay, unusual scripts etc.
- Does the email clearly explain what they do?
- Soft/Valuable CTA?
