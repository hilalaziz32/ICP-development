# Cold E-mail Swipe File:

## Standard e-mail template
Hey {{first name}},
[relevance / problem hypothesis]
[value prop]
[offer / CTA]
[P.S. social proof] 

## Observation > question > solution/proof
Subject - {{problem/concern}}
{{firstname}}, {{observation}}.
{{probing_question}}? {{social_proof}}.
{{CTA}}

**e.g**
Subj: wasted ad budget
Hi Sarah, I saw you’re spending over $60k p/month on paid ads.
Would it be helpful to identify areas where you can cut wasted ad spend and save time?
Personio increased ROAS by 23% after working implementing our ad analytics platform.
Worth exploring?
Speak soon,

## Poke the bear:
Hey {{first_name}} – {{relevant first line}}
{{poke the bear question}}
We’re helping companies like {{case study company_name}} achieve {{outcome}} in {{timeframe}} without {{no good rotten thing}}. 
Would this be something you’re looking to solve as well?

## Competition Email
Hey {{first_name}} – {{competitor 1}} or {{competitor 2}} seem to be the closest choices customers can choose between from what I can see on Google Maps.
Are you thinking about ways to make the choice easier for those customers?
We’re helping companies like {{company_name}} achieve {{outcome}} in {{timeframe}} without {{no good rotten thing}}. 
Would this be something you’re looking to solve as well?

## Statistic > Question > Solution w/ Proof
**Template**
Subject - {{industry statistic}}...
Body - that's the {{industry_statistic_context}}. {{probing_question_to_poke_the_bear}} {{first name}}?. Would you be open to finding out how we helped {{similar_company/competitor}} achieve {{result}}?
{{yourname}}

**Example:**
40-60%...
That’s how many people never come back to your product after signing up according to Intercom. John, we helped Hoppier improve their activation rates by 30% through contextualized product tours. Would you be open to seeing if we could do the same for you?
